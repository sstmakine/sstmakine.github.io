Metallurgical Analysis

For metallurgical evaluation, battery terminals with two different analysis were chosen . First one with 5±0.3% Sb and Second With 3±0.3 Sb and follow tests were ran for them: 3-1-2-1-Metallography
3-1-2-2-Hardness Test
3-1-2-3-Potentiodynamic test
Samples were specified as follow:

Samples were cut, mounted, brushed with 400 to 3000 sand paper and finally polished as below. Then they were chemically etched in Ammonium Molybdant + Citric acid + Distillated water solution .

Hardness Test:

Hardness measuring device : Vickers's Hardness Tester
- Test process
- Cut the terminal vertically.
- Mount the cut sample into Epoxy Resin.
- Polish the terminal cross section.
- Do etch with etching solution
( Acetic Acid 3: Hydrogen Peroxide 1) for 5 seconds and flush with alcohol.
- Measure the hardness at least 10 points with 500g weight and calculate the average.

Petantiodynamic Test:

For electrochemical analysis, after the establishment of the connection as follows, samples were evaluated in a 5 molar solution of sulphuric acid by potantiodynamic test.
