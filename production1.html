High performance cold forge terminal making system

SST MAKINE High performance terminal adopts the cold forging method and has a special anti-rotation design. The produced parts are bright and shiny, and their hardness is much higher in compare with other battery terminal making methods that will help them to meet the OEM customers needs. SST MAKINE High performance cold forge terminal design can prevent from any leakage that is common for other production methods products. This terminal production system completely eliminate the undesirable phenomena and defects such as terminal crack , interior pores, cold spots, product blisters and etc. All qualification tests including Corrosion tests, torque tests, and hardness tests meet the standard requirements that significantly increases our products lifetime and improve the product quality to the international advanced level.
